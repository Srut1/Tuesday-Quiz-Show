Welcome to the Tuesday Quiz Show!!

We welcome all contestants to our highly anticipated, ever popular quiz show!

    Gameplay
  • There will be 3 stages of difficulty with 4 questions each, with increasing difficulty questions giving more points and less time to answer them.
  • Easy: 15 seconds and 25 points
  • Medium: 10 seconds and 50 points
  • Hard: 7 seconds and 100 points
  • But beware! Getting a single answer wrong, or letting the timer run out results in a loss.
  • The top 5 contestants will be shown on the Highscore Board!
